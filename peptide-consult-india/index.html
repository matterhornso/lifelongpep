Why a consult-first model matters

Peptides are often discussed like consumer wellness products. lifelongpep is being built around a different premise: medical judgement first, access only if appropriate, and transparent pricing before purchase.

Medical screening

A consult should consider goals, history, medications, bloodwork, and whether a peptide is appropriate at all.

Partner fulfillment

If prescribed after launch, fulfillment would happen through appropriately licensed partners against a valid prescription.

Protocol tracking

lifelongpep plans to connect protocols and recovery sessions to wearables through openwearables.io.
