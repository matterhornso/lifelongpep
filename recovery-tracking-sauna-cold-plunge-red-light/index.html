Recovery

Recovery tracking: sauna, cold plunge, red light

Recovery routines are easier to evaluate when they are logged. The goal is not perfection; it is a clearer record.

Wearable recovery tracking is a personal logging convenience. It is not diagnosis, treatment, or medical monitoring.

Why track recovery?

People often remember how they felt, but not what they actually did. A log helps connect routines, timing, adherence, and follow-up questions.

What can be tracked?

lifelongpep plans to support tracking for sauna, cold plunge, red light therapy, salt baths, steam, and other recovery sessions as the partner network develops.

Where this fits

Recovery bookings are planned after the metabolic longevity waitlist and GLP-1 readiness workflow. The same platform model should make the activity easier to book, pay for, and log.
