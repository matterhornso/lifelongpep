About

Longevity needs more supervision.

lifelongpep is being built for people in India who want a clearer, doctor-gated path through longevity consults, peptide questions, recovery, supplements, and wearable tracking.

The problem

Longevity is becoming easier to discover and harder to trust. People can find peptide protocols, supplement stacks, recovery routines, and wearable metrics everywhere, but the workflow is fragmented and often unsupervised.

The model

lifelongpep is pre-launch. The intended model separates medical judgement, partner fulfillment, transparent pricing, and personal tracking so users can understand what is happening before they pay or proceed.

Consult first

A doctor-gated model should start with goals, history, labs where available, and whether an intervention is appropriate.

Partners matter

Any prescribed fulfillment after launch is intended to run through appropriately licensed partners.

Track clearly

Protocol and recovery logs should be easier to remember, discuss, and review.

Current status: waitlist mode. No consults, prescriptions, payments, or fulfillment are available through this site today.
