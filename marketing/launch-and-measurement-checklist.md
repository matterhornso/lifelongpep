# Launch and measurement checklist

## Owner actions

### 1. Activate FormSubmit

FormSubmit has already received a test submission attempt and sent an activation email to `hello@lifelongpep.fit`.

Steps:

1. Open the inbox for `hello@lifelongpep.fit`.
2. Search for `FormSubmit` or `Activate Form`.
3. Open the newest activation email.
4. Click the activation link.
5. After activation, submit a real test from `https://lifelongpep.fit/`.
6. Confirm the email arrives with subject similar to `lifelongpep waitlist signup`.

If you do not see the email:

1. Check spam/promotions.
2. Submit the live homepage form once from a browser.
3. Search again for `FormSubmit`.
