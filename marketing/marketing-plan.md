# lifelongpep Marketing Plan

## Positioning

lifelongpep is a doctor-gated longevity marketplace being built for India. The current website should sell trust, clarity, and early access rather than active booking. The core contrast is supervised, accountable longevity workflows versus gray-market peptides, fragmented recovery services, and self-directed tracking.

## Current status

- Pre-launch waitlist
- India-wide positioning
- Provider and partner network in progress
- No consult booking, prescriptions, payments, or fulfillment through the site today
- Primary inbox: hello@lifelongpep.fit

## Primary audiences

- People researching peptides who want medical supervision
- People seeking longevity or preventive health consults in India
- Doctors in longevity, preventive medicine, endocrinology, sports medicine, and recovery
- Licensed fulfillment, diagnostics, recovery, and wellness partners
- Journalists, creators, and operators following longevity in India

## Funnel

1. Awareness: SEO pages, issues, founder-led social, partner conversations
2. Education: plain-language issues, FAQ, transparent model explanation
3. Capture: homepage waitlist and issue subscription
4. Qualification: reply workflows by intent: patient, doctor, partner, press
5. Launch: notify waitlist when consult booking opens

## First-wave priorities

- Keep homepage pre-launch and trust-forward
- Build India-wide SEO pages for high-intent searches
- Publish issues that educate without hype
- Capture separate intent streams for waitlist, issues, doctors, and partners
- Add analytics events before paid traffic

## Next campaigns

- Founder-led LinkedIn posts about supervised longevity in India
- Instagram carousels explaining peptide myths and consult workflows
- Partner outreach to doctors, recovery studios, diagnostics, and fulfillment operators
- Search-focused educational articles
- Launch announcement to waitlist once booking is ready
