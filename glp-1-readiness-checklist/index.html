Checklist

Before GLP-1, get ready.

A readiness consult should make the medical conversation clearer before any prescription decision is made.

This checklist is educational only. It does not decide eligibility, recommend medication, or replace a doctor.

Bring these to the conversation

  • Your goal. Weight, waist, energy, blood sugar, appetite, mobility, long-term maintenance, or another measurable outcome.
  • Your history. Diagnoses, prior weight-loss attempts, medication list, supplements, allergies, family history, and current symptoms.
  • Bloodwork context. Recent routine labs or doctor-ordered panels if available. Do not order labs from this page.
  • Muscle plan. Protein intake, resistance training, injury constraints, strength baseline, and recovery routine.
  • Side-effect plan. GI symptoms, hydration, nutrition, dose changes, escalation, and when to contact a doctor.
  • Cost and continuity. Monthly affordability, follow-up timing, maintenance expectations, and what happens if treatment stops.

How lifelongpep uses this

lifelongpep is building a doctor-gated workflow where readiness information helps a future consult start with context, not guesswork.
