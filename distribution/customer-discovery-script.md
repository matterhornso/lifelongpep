# Customer discovery script

Goal: speak with 10 people who are considering GLP-1, have used it, stopped it, or are researching metabolic health.

## Opening

I'm researching how people in India think about GLP-1 readiness and metabolic longevity. This is not medical advice and I am not selling anything on this call.

## Questions

1. What made you start researching GLP-1 or metabolic health?
2. What are you hoping changes?
3. What worries you?
4. Who would you trust for advice?
5. What would make you distrust a platform immediately?
6. What do you already know about side effects?
7. Have you thought about muscle loss, protein, or resistance training?
8. Have you thought about what happens after stopping?
9. What would you want prepared before speaking to a doctor?
10. Would you join a waitlist for a doctor-gated readiness consult?

## Capture fields

- Name
- City
- Persona: researching / currently using / stopped / doctor-referred / partner-referred
- Main goal
- Main fear
- Trust trigger
- Objection
- Quote
- Follow-up permission

## Turn objections into pages

Every repeated objection should become a page, FAQ, or founder post.
