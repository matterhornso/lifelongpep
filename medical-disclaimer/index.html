Medical disclaimer

Do not use this website as a substitute for advice from a qualified medical professional.

No medical advice

Content on this website is educational and informational. It should not be treated as medical advice or a recommendation to start, stop, or change any treatment, medication, supplement, peptide, recovery routine, or health protocol.

No doctor-patient relationship

Using this website, reading an issue, joining the waitlist, or emailing lifelongpep does not create a doctor-patient relationship.

No prescriptions or fulfillment today

Consult booking, prescriptions, payments, and fulfillment are not available through this site today. Any future prescription decision would need to be made independently by a qualified doctor.

Wearables

Consumer wearables are not medical devices. Planned wearable logging is intended as a personal tracking convenience, not a diagnostic input or clinical monitor.
