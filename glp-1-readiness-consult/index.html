GLP-1 readiness, before access.

lifelongpep is pre-launch. The first clinical lane is a doctor-gated readiness workflow for people in India considering GLP-1 or related metabolic therapy.

Important: this page does not sell GLP-1 medicine, provide medical advice, or guarantee a prescription. It explains the consult workflow lifelongpep is building.

What readiness means

GLP-1 readiness is the work before any prescription decision: goals, body composition, medical history, contraindications, current medication, prior weight-loss attempts, bloodwork context, nutrition, training, and follow-up expectations.

What a doctor should review

Eligibility

Whether a GLP-1 or related metabolic therapy is clinically appropriate for the person and goal.

Risk

Contraindications, side-effect history, medication interactions, pregnancy plans, and red flags.

Muscle preservation

Protein intake, resistance training, strength baseline, and avoiding weight loss that sacrifices lean mass.

Continuation plan

How progress, side effects, adherence, discontinuation, and maintenance should be discussed.

Where lifelongpep fits

lifelongpep is being built as the orchestration layer: waitlist, consult booking when live, partner workflows where legally and clinically appropriate, and wearable-based protocol tracking. Medical judgement stays with qualified doctors.
