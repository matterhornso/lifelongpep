Why supervision matters

Peptides are often discussed casually, but the decision to use one should consider evidence, risks, contraindications, source quality, and whether the goal is medically reasonable.

What doctor-gated means

Doctor-gated means a qualified doctor decides independently whether an intervention is appropriate. The platform should not guarantee access or pressure a prescription outcome.

What fulfillment should look like

If prescribed after launch, fulfillment is intended to happen through appropriately licensed partners against a valid prescription. Pricing and platform fees should be visible before purchase.
