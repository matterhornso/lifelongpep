A careful doctor network, before booking opens.

lifelongpep is building a provider network for metabolic longevity and GLP-1 readiness in India. Consult booking is not live yet.

We cannot claim doctors are onboarded until they are. This page explains the standards and conversations being built.

What the network should value

Independent judgement

Doctors decide whether GLP-1, another intervention, further testing, or no prescription is appropriate.

Metabolic context

Goals, labs where available, medication history, body composition, muscle, sleep, and recovery matter.

Follow-up discipline

Side effects, affordability, maintenance, discontinuation, and progress should be discussed over time.

For doctors

We are interested in endocrinology, obesity medicine, internal medicine, preventive medicine, sports medicine, and careful adjacent practices.
