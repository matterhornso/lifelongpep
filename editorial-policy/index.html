Editorial policy

lifelongpep publishes educational content to help people ask better questions before pursuing longevity interventions.

Content should clarify, not sell certainty. When evidence is limited, the page should say so.

Standards

We avoid guaranteed outcomes, miracle language, and claims that make peptides, supplements, recovery tools, or wearables sound risk-free. We distinguish planned platform features from live services.

Medical boundaries

Issues and articles are not medical advice. They should encourage readers to speak with qualified professionals rather than self-prescribe or source interventions without supervision.

Updates

Pages should be updated when product status, launch status, partner availability, pricing, or medical-adjacent claims change.
