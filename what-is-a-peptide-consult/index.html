Peptide education

What is a peptide consult?

A peptide consult is a doctor-led conversation about whether peptide use is appropriate for your goals, history, risk profile, and the evidence behind a specific intervention.

Short answer: a peptide consult should help decide whether a peptide is appropriate at all. It is not a guaranteed path to a prescription.

What gets discussed

A useful consult should cover your goals, medical history, medications, prior diagnoses, bloodwork where available, sleep, recovery, stress, training load, and what you have already tried.

What the doctor decides

The doctor decides independently whether any intervention is appropriate. Sometimes the answer may be a peptide. Sometimes it may be lifestyle changes, follow-up testing, a different intervention, or no medical intervention.
